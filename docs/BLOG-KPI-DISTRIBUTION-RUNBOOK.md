# Blog KPI and Distribution Runbook

## Objective

Turn rewritten blog content into a measurable growth engine for:

- organic reach
- engagement quality
- blog-to-product conversion

## KPI dashboard (weekly)

Track per post and aggregate:

- impressions
- clicks
- CTR
- average engagement time
- scroll depth trend
- blog -> `/try` CTR
- lead-magnet submission rate
- internal click-through to templates/examples

## KPI targets (90-day)

- Blog-to-`/try` CTR >= 3.5%
- Non-India English organic share: 15-25%
- 100% of 12 posts aligned to copy architecture standard
- Upward trend in engagement metrics on rewritten posts

## Weekly operating cadence

1. Export previous 7-day metrics.
2. Identify top 3 underperforming posts by CTR or engagement.
3. Update intro hook, title phrasing, meta description, and CTA copy.
4. Validate internal links and related paths.
5. Republish and annotate change log.

## Distribution pack per post

For each rewritten article:

- 1 LinkedIn post variant
- 1 newsletter snippet
- 1 community summary version
- UTM-tagged destination links

## Monthly review

- Which posts drive best conversion?
- Which topics attract highest non-India traffic?
- Which CTA variants win by intent class?
- Which internal link paths produce strongest assist conversions?
