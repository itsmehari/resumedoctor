Step 1 — Land
Landing page with stats, templates, testimonials, pricing teaser
Step 2 — Auth
Email signup, Google/GitHub OAuth, or Try Free with OTP — no card needed
Step 3 — Template
30 templates × 7 visual variant axes = 210 possible looks
Step 4 — Build
14 section types, drag-to-reorder, live preview synced in real time
Step 5 — AI
OpenAI GPT-4 writes bullet points, polishes summary, improves weak lines
Step 6 — ATS
Score 0–100, keyword gaps highlighted, one-click fixes suggested
Step 7 — Export
PDF (free), DOCX (Pro), TXT for ATS paste. Every export is logged.
Step 8 — Apply
Naukri, LinkedIn, Indeed India, Internshala, TimesJobs, Shine, Foundit

USER
Central entity. Owns all resumes, cover letters, and applications. Plan field gates Pro features.
RESUME
Stores full ResumeSection[] as JSON. Template ID links to the 30 registered templates.
EXPORT_LOG
Every PDF/DOCX/TXT download is timestamped and IP-logged for abuse prevention and analytics.
TRIAL_SESSION
24-hour no-signup sessions. OTP-authenticated. converted: true when user signs up.
