Chennai’s job market spans IT, manufacturing, healthcare, and services. Your resume should be clear, ATS-friendly, and tailored to the roles you target.

## Get the Basics Right

- **Contact:** Use a professional email and a single phone number. Add LinkedIn if it’s updated.
- **Location:** “Chennai, Tamil Nadu” or “Chennai, India” is enough. No need for full address on the resume.
- **Photo:** Optional in India. If you add one, use a neutral, professional headshot.

## Tailor by Industry

**IT / Tech:** Emphasise technologies, projects, and impact (e.g. “Built APIs serving 10K+ requests/day”). Use standard headings: Experience, Education, Skills, Projects.

**Manufacturing / Operations:** Highlight safety, efficiency, and scale. Include certifications (e.g. Six Sigma) if relevant.

**BFSI / Services:** Focus on compliance, client handling, and process improvement. Keep language formal.

## Use Job Portals Wisely

- **Naukri, LinkedIn, Indeed:** Upload a clean PDF. Ensure your headline and summary match the roles you apply for.
- **Company career pages:** Often ask for Word or PDF. Use an [ATS-friendly template](/templates) so formatting stays intact.

## Chennai to global pathway (optional)

Many Chennai professionals eventually target remote/global teams. To prepare:

- Use globally understandable role titles
- Add outcome metrics in your bullets
- Highlight collaboration tools (Jira, Slack, GitHub, Notion)
- Keep LinkedIn profile aligned with resume claims

You can be locally strong and globally discoverable at the same time.

## Quick Chennai application checklist

- [ ] Resume headline includes role + key stack/domain
- [ ] Top bullet reflects role impact in your target industry
- [ ] Naukri and LinkedIn titles match resume
- [ ] Notice period and location settings are updated
